The Founder

Built by a manicurist who kept mixing her own color.

Pretty Ink started at a table in Pontiac, Michigan, where Deneen Butler — twenty years into a salon career — stopped waiting for the shade she wanted and began making it herself. First for clients. Then for friends. Then for a city.

Every bottle still passes through her hands before it ships. The brand is Black woman-owned, independent, and still small enough that the person who made the polish answers your email.

— Deneen Butler
Founder · Pontiac, Michigan · Est. 2023
The Ritual

Five steps.
Three weeks.

Slow the application down and it holds up. Here is the house method, in order.

01
Prep.

Shape, buff the shine off the nail, wipe clean with a lint-free pad and dehydrator.

02
Base.

One thin coat of Pretty Ink base. Cap the free edge. Cure 60s LED or 120s UV.

03
Color.

Two thin coats, each cured. Three for lighter shades. Patience over thickness.

04
Top.

No-wipe top coat. Full cure. No acetone, no residue — just shine.

05
Oil.

Cuticle oil into each bed. Done — and three weeks to not think about it.
